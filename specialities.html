Swimming Pool and Children's Playground

FACILITIES
  • Main Swimming Pool

Well Furnished Rooms

LUXURY AND COMFORT

Hotel Sai Leela has elegantly furnished, both air-conditioned and non air-conditioned rooms realizing the varying weather Shirdi experiences.

We realize that our guests need comfortable beds clean rooms & fresh linen after their journey. Our rooms are designed to be comfortable and homely.

Best in Class Amenities

TEMPLE PICKUP & DROP, TOURIST GUIDANCE AND MORE

  • Baggage storage while you are waiting for check-in or after you have checked-out.
  • Pick up & drop facility for Sai temple at no additional cost.
  • Guidance on temple facilities, timings, information on important temple events, entry to temple etc
