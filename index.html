EXPRESSION OF FAITH

Shirdi is well known for the holy soul of Shri Sachidanand Sadguru Sainath Maharaj - affectionately known as Saibaba rests today 'SAI BABA' (Information Book). Finding good, clean & reasonable accommodation in shirdi has never been easy for the Devotees. Hotel Sai Leela strives hard to provide the best hotel services to their guests. We at Hotel Sai Leela provide you luxury and affordable accommodation at Shirdi and that too at economical prices. Our hotel is a renowned budget hotel established in 1989 in the category of budget hotels in Shirdi. We offer our customers outstanding personal services complimented with charm and comfort.
